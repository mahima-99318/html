About Sir C.R. Reddy College


Sir C.R. Reddy College is a prestigious educational institution located in Eluru, Andhra Pradesh, India. It was established in 1945 and has since been providing quality education to students in various fields. The college offers undergraduate and postgraduate courses in arts, science, commerce, and professional studies. It is known for its dedicated faculty, excellent infrastructure, and a vibrant campus life that fosters holistic development among students.
